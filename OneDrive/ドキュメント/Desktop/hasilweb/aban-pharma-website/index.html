About ABAN Pharmaceuticals

Dedicated to Advancing Healthcare Through Quality & Innovation

ABAN Pharmaceuticals is a trusted name in the pharmaceutical industry, committed to delivering high-quality healthcare products. With decades of experience since 1981, we specialize in manufacturing and supplying a wide range of pharmaceutical formulations.

Our expertise includes tablets, capsules, syrups, and nutraceutical products. We maintain the highest standards of quality control to ensure that every product meets international safety and efficacy guidelines.
